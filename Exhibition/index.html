X-HIBIT

CALL FOR POSTER (X-hibit) Posters are a great way to display innovative ideas or a work in progress. We are thus calling for your submissions in form of a poster to present in an exhibition which will be visited by eminent personalities from various research fields as well as the tech industry.

You're welcome to makes posters on your projects be it complete or a work in progress, and also pitch innovative ideas on products, services or entrepreneurship ventures. For participating in the exhibition you need to first submit an abstract of the content you'd be presenting on.

After screening the abstracts, the most original and new submissions would be shortlisted to be exhibited in the tech-fest on 22nd January, 2023.

Your posters in the exhibition will not only be marketed to students but also to dignitaries across academia and research and guests of honour. The best ideas among the lot would stand a chance to win exciting rewards and recognition at the fest. Grand prizes worth 15k await!

Abstract submission deadline is 15th January, 2023 (EOD)..
